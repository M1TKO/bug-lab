Data privacy and security

Real privacy

No third-party tracking scripts from companies collecting, profiling, and selling consumer data. Targeting is carefully calibrated to ensure a balance between data privacy and ad relevance. No personal information is stored on the blockchain.

Full transparency

No more ads with unknown sources and scripts running malicious code, malware, and ransomware. Our Unique Hash IDs introduce accountability and eliminate the vast majority of ad fraud schemes. Anyone can check the hash of any ad creative and find out who is paying for it.

Enhanced control over the user experience

Removing undesirable ads

You can quickly and easily remove ads you consider inappropriate via the Flag or Block options accessible from the icon located at the top right corner of every ad served through the AdHash Protocol. On top of that, a comprehensive database of all hosted advertising assets is available to and readable by anyone. Not only will you be contributing to the quality of the browsing experience of everyone else but you will also be rewarded for it. For more information on flagging and blocking, please visit our Help page.

Seamless user experience

No more disruptive ad formats and ad repetition on the same page. AdHash uses only static ads which do not distract from the content and are less heavy on your hardware. This, together with our algorithmic optimisations and the removal of all third-party scripts, contribute to much quicker page loading times.

What do I need to get started?

1. Complete the join form for voters.

2. Follow the steps from your email to log into the AdHash Voting Platform.

3. Once logged in, you can see your current rank. It is 25 points for newly registered voters. For more information on ranks, please visit our Voting page.

4. When selected to vote, you will receive an email request containing a link. The link is only valid for 30 minutes before it expires. When you click on the link, it will lead you to the Voting Platform where you can review the case and submit your vote. From your profile, you can see all past and pending votes.

5. Once the final vote is complete, you will receive an email notification containing the result, your new rank, and the amount of voting rewards earned (if applicable).
